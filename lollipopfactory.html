Lollipop Factory is yet another duo band that debuted in 1997 (WOW! what a great year!). I'd describe their sound as an excellent hyrbid of Queen and Primus, if that makes sense. This band is underrated as hell, I only discovered it from one of my father's CDs, and I'm glad I did.

Sick Animation (aka Marc M) is an animator, musician, and voice actor who makes funny internet cartoons. Some of his joke songs are actually pretty good.

QUICK SONG REVIEWS


Since sick animation is mostly joke songs, i don't really wanna waste my time reviewing full albums. Instead I'm choosing a few of my favorite songs to review.

When I Lay You Down

The Gorillionaires are back with one more studio album before they retire y'all. It's a super catchy song that I now know all of the words to after listening to it for so long. Every verse is really well written, as expected from Marc M. I have to say third verse is so good and definitely the best. 10/10

Summer Rain

A cute song about meeting a girl. It sounds like a nice acoustic love song at first but once you listen to the actual words it changes completely into Marc's raunchy style that I find very entertaining. 9/10

Skating Boards

This one's from his most recent album, and I like it quite a bit. Reminds me of my fond memories of doing skating boards with my best friends 8/10

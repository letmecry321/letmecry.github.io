Twelve Hour Turn is a hardcore/screamo band from Jacksonville Florida. They were active between 1996-2002. Active around the Jacksonville and Gainsville area, they were contemporary with I Hate Myself, and even made a split album with them entitled... split. As far as I know they aren't currently active aside from the occasional live show in FL and whoever runs their Facebook account.

Ween was started by Aaron Freeman (Gener) and Mickey Melchiondo (Deaner) in 1984. They are able to competently replicate/parody any genre they like, making each album and even every song completely different. Their style will go from R&B to psychedelic to funk and even country. 10/10 band to huff Scotchgard to. Though their choice of genre will vary, they can definitely be described as... "weird". They will come up with the stupidest idea for a song and then go on to put their full effort into making said song as good as possible.
